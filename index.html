The studio

One woman. One window.
Oil on linen.

She has been painting since before she could explain why. Komorebi is the studio she works from — a single room, north-facing, where every painting is made slowly and by hand.

The studio takes private commissions, ships internationally, and is open to gallery and curatorial inquiries. Originals are one of one. Limited prints are made to archival standard, signed and numbered.
